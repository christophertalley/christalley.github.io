About me

I'm Chris and I am from Birmingham, AL. Ive always been interested in technology, but it wasnt until I saw the effect that automation had on our everyday lives that I decided to learn to program, and I haven't looked back since.

Solving problems is one of my biggest assets and a huge part of why I am a great software engineer. Some of my other strengths include leadership, teamwork, and being an adept learner. Before coming into this field, my skillset led me to several interesting jobs in the Civil Engineering industry that all contributed to who I am today.

On a personal note, I love watching and playing sports. Participating in sports when I was younger helped build a solid basis of teamwork from an early age. When I'm not coding or watching sports, I am taking my dog on an adventure or playing video games with my friends.

It is very important to me to always be learning. Whether it is technical or not, there is something about learning a new thing that fills me with excitement. I am currently self-learning MongoDB, and plan on moving to other technologies such as Angular, Vue, Django, and any other frameworks that catch my eye along the way.
